About Avinya

Our Mission

Our AI-powered education platform transforms learning by converting text, notes, and lectures into engaging video lessons with AI avatars, animations, and voice narration. Students can access personalized study materials, generate AI-powered notes and summaries, and get instant doubt-solving through AI tutors. With interactive quizzes, gamification, and AI-generated study schedules, learning becomes more efficient and enjoyable. Additionally, students can connect with top educators through 1-on-1 video calls for expert guidance. Seamlessly integrated with YouTube, LMS platforms, and custom educational apps, our platform ensures a smarter, more accessible, and engaging learning experience for all.
